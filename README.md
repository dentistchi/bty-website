# bty - Better Than Yesterday: Dr. Chi's Dental Education Platform

A premium, Apple-inspired educational website designed for dental professionals seeking comprehensive growth in clinical skills, patient management, team relationships, and financial wisdom—all grounded in the bty (Better Than Yesterday) mindset philosophy.

## 🌟 Project Overview

**Journey with Clinical Director Dr. Chi** is a holistic educational platform that goes beyond technical dentistry to address the complete professional development of dental practitioners. The philosophy centers on self-improvement, compassion, and continuous growth rather than comparison with others.

## 💡 Core Philosophy

**Better Than Yesterday (bty)** is not about competing with others. It's about:
- **Self-Improvement:** Measuring progress against your past self

- **Daily Practice:** Small, consistent improvements compound over time
