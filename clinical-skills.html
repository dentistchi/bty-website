Dental Filling

Learn proper cavity preparation, material selection, and restoration techniques for optimal results.

Pro Tips

  • Always ensure complete caries removal before filling
  • Use proper isolation techniques for moisture control
  • Layer composite materials in 2mm increments
  • Check occlusion carefully before final polish

Crown Preparation

Master the art of crown preparation including proper reduction, margin design, and impression techniques.

Pro Tips

  • Ensure 1.5-2mm occlusal reduction for adequate strength
  • Create smooth, flowing lines without sharp angles
  • Maintain proper taper (6-10 degrees)
  • Use depth-cutting burs for consistent reduction

Tooth Extraction

Develop safe extraction techniques, proper instrument selection, and post-operative care protocols.

Pro Tips

  • Always assess root morphology on radiographs first
  • Use proper luxation before forceps application
  • Apply controlled, consistent pressure
  • Inspect socket for retained root fragments

Root Canal Treatment

Perfect endodontic techniques including access cavity, cleaning, shaping, and obturation methods.

Pro Tips

  • Create straight-line access to canal orifices
  • Use rubber dam isolation for all cases
  • Confirm working length with apex locator and radiograph
  • Irrigate copiously with NaOCl between instruments

Dental Implant

Learn implant placement protocols, site assessment, and achieving optimal primary stability.

Pro Tips

  • Assess bone quality and quantity with CBCT
  • Maintain 3D implant positioning guidelines
  • Use sequential drilling for proper osteotomy
  • Achieve >35 Ncm torque for immediate loading

Bone Graft

Master bone augmentation techniques for implant site development and ridge preservation.

Pro Tips

  • Choose graft material based on defect type
  • Ensure primary closure for predictable healing
  • Hydrate particulate grafts before placement
  • Allow 4-6 months healing before implant placement

Sinus Lift

Advanced sinus augmentation techniques via lateral window and crestal approaches.

Pro Tips

  • Evaluate sinus anatomy and pathology on CBCT
  • Use piezoelectric instruments to minimize membrane perforation
  • Elevate membrane gently and completely
  • Place implants simultaneously if >4mm bone height

Ridge Split

Learn ridge expansion techniques for narrow alveolar ridges to enable implant placement.

Pro Tips

  • Ensure minimum 3mm ridge width before splitting
  • Use osteotomes progressively to expand bone
  • Maintain buccal cortical plate integrity
  • Consider staged approach for severely atrophic ridges

GBR - Guided Bone Regeneration

Master membrane techniques for predictable bone regeneration around implants and defects.

Pro Tips

  • Select appropriate membrane (resorbable vs. non-resorbable)
  • Ensure membrane extends 2-3mm beyond defect margins
  • Stabilize membrane with tacks or sutures
  • Achieve tension-free primary closure

Wisdom Teeth Extraction

Surgical removal of impacted third molars with proper sectioning and bone management techniques.

Pro Tips

  • Classify impaction using Pell & Gregory system
  • Create adequate flap design for access and visibility
  • Section tooth strategically to minimize bone removal
  • Protect lingual nerve during lower third molar surgery

IV Sedation

Safe administration of intravenous conscious sedation for anxious patients and complex procedures.

Pro Tips

  • Complete thorough pre-operative medical assessment
  • Establish reliable IV access and secure catheter
  • Titrate medications slowly to desired sedation level
  • Monitor vital signs continuously throughout procedure
  • Have emergency drugs and equipment readily available

All-on-6 Procedures

Full-arch rehabilitation using six strategically placed implants for immediate function and aesthetics.

Pro Tips

  • Use surgical guide for precise implant positioning
  • Distribute implants evenly for optimal load distribution
  • Angle posterior implants to avoid anatomical structures
  • Ensure all implants achieve >35 Ncm for immediate loading
  • Fabricate provisional prosthesis before surgery
