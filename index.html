Daily Practice Tips

1

Morning Reflection

Start your day by acknowledging one thing you're grateful for about yourself. Write it down or say it aloud.

Tip: Keep a gratitude journal by your bedside
2

Mindful Interaction

When someone frustrates you, pause for 3 breaths. Ask yourself: "What might they be going through?"

Tip: Empathy transforms conflict into understanding
3

Help, Don't Judge

Before criticizing, ask: "How can I help this person?" Shift from judgment to support.

Tip: Your words have power to uplift or diminish
4

Evening Review

End your day by identifying one way you were better than yesterday. Celebrate small wins.

Tip: Progress, not perfection, is the goal
